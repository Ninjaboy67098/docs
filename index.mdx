## What is Estizee?

**Estizee** is an all-in-one service business management platform designed specifically for **field service professionals**.

## Workflow

It streamlines the entire workflow—from **instant job estimates** to **scheduling, invoicing, payments, and customer communication**—so service businesses can operate efficiently, scale faster, and deliver a modern customer experience.

## Why We Built Estizee

Estizee was built with real-world service businesses in mind, eliminating manual processes, fragmented tools, and guesswork.

## Who Estizee Is For

Estizee is ideal for:

- Window cleaning businesses
- Pressure washing companies
- Residential & commercial service providers
- Solo operators and growing teams
- Service businesses that want automation without complexity

Whether you’re a single operator or managing multiple crews, Estizee adapts to your workflow.

## Core Philosophy

Estizee is built around three core principles:

**1. Automation without friction**\
Reduce admin work by automating estimates, reminders, invoices, and follow-ups—without sacrificing control.

**2. Customer-first experience**\
Customers get clear quotes, fast approvals, transparent pricing, and smooth scheduling.

**3. Business intelligence built-in**\
Every quote, job, and invoice becomes actionable data to help you grow smarter.

## What Problems Estizee Solves

Estizee replaces:

- Manual quoting and on-site estimates
- Back-and-forth scheduling calls and texts
- Disconnected invoicing and payment tools
- Missed follow-ups and forgotten reminders
- Poor customer visibility into job status

With Estizee, everything lives in one system.

## Key Capabilities at a Glance

- **Instant Estimates & Quotes**\
  Turbo and Advanced quote engines with dynamic pricing logic and surcharges.
- **Online Booking & Scheduling**\
  Calendar-based job scheduling with availability control.
- **Customer & Job Management**\
  Centralized customer profiles, job history, and service details.
- **Invoices & Payments**\
  Automatic quote-to-invoice conversion with Stripe integration.
- **Notifications & Reminders**\
  SMS and email reminders for scheduled, completed, and paid jobs.
- **Admin Controls & Pricing Rules**\
  Fully configurable base rates, service rules, and business settings.

## How Estizee Works (High Level)

1. **Customer requests a quote** via your website or admin panel
2. **Estizee generates a price** using your business rules
3. **Customer approves the quote**
4. **Job is scheduled** based on availability
5. **Invoice is generated automatically**
6. **Customer pays online**
7. **Follow-ups and reminders are sent automatically**

No manual steps. No disconnected tools.

## Designed for Growth

Estizee is designed to grow with your business:

- Start simple as a solo operator
- Add employees and teams
- Introduce advanced pricing and scheduling rules
- Enable customer portals
- Expand to multiple service types

You don’t outgrow Estizee—Estizee scales with you.
