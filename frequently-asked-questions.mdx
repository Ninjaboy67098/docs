## **Getting Started**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Estizee?">
    Estizee is an all-in-one business management platform built specifically for window cleaning and exterior service companies. It helps automate quoting, scheduling, invoicing, payments, and customer management.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is Estizee designed for?">
    Estizee is built for window cleaners, pressure washing businesses, and exterior service professionals who want to streamline operations and reduce manual administrative work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need technical experience to use Estizee?">
    No. Estizee is designed for service professionals and can be used without technical knowledge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does it take to get started?">
    Most businesses can complete setup and begin sending quotes within 15–30 minutes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can beginners use Estizee if they don’t know how to price jobs yet?">
    Yes. Estizee includes guided onboarding and pricing presets to help new businesses establish pricing quickly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## **Jobs and Scheduling**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can customers book appointments themselves?">
    Customers can select their preferred dates for their job in the client approval portal process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Estizee sync with Google Calendar?">
    Yes. Estizee does sync with Google Calendar. You can see how to access that [here](jobs-and-scheduling/google-calendar-sync).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can jobs be rescheduled or edited easily?">
    Jobs can be rescheduled by just changing the date and time through the jobs page. The editing drawers are found in the job details pages when you click on one of the accordions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are customer reminders sent automatically?">
    Customer reminders are sent automatically prior to the job. The amount of hours prior to the job for it to be sent is set by you as the user. SMS also has to be enabled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I view upcoming jobs and daily schedules in one place?">
    You can see your upcoming jobs on the home page and on the jobs page and it is the same to see your daily schedules in one place.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## **Invoices and Payments**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are invoices created in Estizee?">
    Invoices can be made from existing jobs or created manually. From existing jobs, you would just click create invoice once the job is marked as completed. To make a manual invoice you would need to click the big + button at the bottom and then, click invoice. This is in the mobile version of the app. In the web version it would be the same type of button but on the top left of the sidebar.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Estizee support online payments?">
    Yes. Estizee supports online payments through the Stripe checkout.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
    The payment methods that are supported are credit card and offline payment methods.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can customers pay directly from their phone?">
    Customers can pay directly from their phone through the client/customer portal.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are receipts sent automatically after payment?">
    No. Receipts are not automatically after payment.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I track unpaid invoices?">
    You can track unpaid invoices in the invoices page of the app. You can select the unpaid filter so that you can only see your unpaid invoices.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
