---
title: "Turbo Quote Vs. Advanced Quote"
description: "Estizee offers two quoting options designed to balance speed and detail: Turbo Quote and Advanced Quote."
---

Each serves a specific purpose depending on the job complexity and customer needs.

## **Turbo Quote**

**Turbo Quote** is designed for quick estimates with minimal input. It is ideal for:

- Fast residential quotes
- On-the-spot quoting during calls or site visits
- Scenarios where speed is more important than detailed customization

**Features:**

- Requires only 3-4 key inputs
- Automatically calculates base pricing
- Includes standard add-ons and service options
- Can be shared instantly with customers via PDF, email, or SMS

## **Advanced Quote**

**Advanced Quote** provides full control over all pricing variables. It is best for:

- Commercial or multi-unit properties
- Complex residential jobs with multiple add-ons
- Jobs requiring manual adjustments or special pricing rules

**Features:**

- Full access to residential and commercial pricing rules
- Customizable add-ons, modifiers, and surcharges
- Supports recurring or multi-service quotes
- Allows admin overrides and manual adjustments

## _Choosing the Right Quote_

- Use **Turbo Quote** for speed and simplicity, ideal for common residential jobs.
- Use **Advanced Quote** when detail, accuracy, or customization is critical, such as commercial properties or complex scenarios.

Both options integrate seamlessly with Estizee’s scheduling, invoicing, and reporting systems, ensuring a consistent workflow from quote creation to payment.
