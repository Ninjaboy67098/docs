Proper job management ensures accurate scheduling, team assignment, and service delivery.

All changes apply to **new and updated jobs**.

## **Manually Creating a Job**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Locate the big + button on the bottom navigation bar.

    ![IMG 2248 Copy 2](/images/IMG_2248copy2.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Select "Create Job".

    ![Screenshot 2026 02 09 At 3 26 53 PM Copy](/images/Screenshot2026-02-09at3.26.53PMcopy.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Enter customer details, service type, and property information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Assign the job to a team member or crew.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    Set the scheduled date, start time, and estimated duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6">
    Review pricing and apply any relevant surcharges or add-ons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7">
    Save the job and it will add it to the calendar and into the jobs page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Editing a Job**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Locate the job in the Calendar or Jobs module.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2">
    Click on the preferred accordion to edit in.

    ![Screenshot 2026 02 09 At 3 28 53 PM Copy](/images/Screenshot2026-02-09at3.28.53PMcopy.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3">
    Update any necessary details, such as customer information, assigned team member, or scheduled time.

    ![Screenshot 2026 02 09 At 3 29 14 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-02-09at3.29.14PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4">
    Adjust pricing, add-ons, or special instructions if required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5">
    Save changes to update the job and notify relevant team members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Best Practices**

- Double-check customer contact information before scheduling.
- Assign jobs according to team availability to prevent conflicts.
- Include notes for special conditions or customer preferences.
- Update jobs promptly if changes occur to keep schedules accurate.

Proper creation and editing of jobs ensures efficient operations, accurate scheduling, and clear communication with both team members and customers in Estizee.
