---

Proper assignment ensures workload balance, accountability, and timely job completion.

All changes apply to **new and updated jobs**.

## **Assigning a Team Member**

1. Navigate to the job in the Calendar or Jobs module.
2. Select "Assign Team Member" or "Edit Assignment".
3. Choose the appropriate team member or crew from the list.
4. Confirm the assignment. The team member will receive notification of the job.

## **Team Availability**

- Ensure that team members are available for the selected time and date.
- Consider existing assignments and buffer times to prevent scheduling conflicts.
- Adjust the assignment if availability changes or conflicts arise.

## **Role-Based Access**

- Only administrators or managers can assign team members.
- Team members can view jobs assigned to them but cannot reassign without permission.

## **Best Practices**

- Assign jobs promptly after scheduling to keep the calendar accurate.
- Balance workload evenly across team members to avoid overloading.
- Include special instructions or notes for team members within the job details.
- Regularly review assignments to optimize efficiency and productivity.

Proper assignment of team members ensures smooth operations, accountability, and accurate tracking of work in Estizee.
