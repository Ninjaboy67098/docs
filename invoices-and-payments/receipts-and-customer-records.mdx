Proper record-keeping ensures accurate financial tracking, easy access to customer history, and compliance with business or legal requirements.

All features apply to **new and existing jobs and invoices**.

## **Receipts**

- **Automatic Generation**: Estizee automatically generates a receipt for every paid invoice.
- **Formats**: Receipts can be viewed online, sent via email, or downloaded as PDFs.
- **Content**: Each receipt includes customer details, job description, payment amount, payment method, and date.

## **Customer Records**

- **Profile Creation**: Customers are automatically added to the system when a job or quote is created.
- **Contact Information**: Stores phone numbers, email addresses, and billing addresses.
- **Job History**: Maintains a history of quotes, jobs, invoices, and payments.
- **Notes & Attachments**: Add notes or files to customer records for future reference.

## **Best Practices**

- Ensure all customer contact information is accurate for proper receipt delivery.
- Regularly review customer records for completeness and correctness.
- Use job history and notes to personalize service and improve customer experience.
- Maintain data privacy and compliance with applicable regulations.

Proper management of receipts and customer records in Estizee ensures transparency, accurate tracking, and enhanced customer relationships.
