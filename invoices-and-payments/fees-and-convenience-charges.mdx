These charges cover payment processing costs, service convenience, or other business-related fees.

All fees apply to **new quotes and invoices**.

## **Common Fees**

- **Convenience Fee**: A small percentage added to cover processing costs for online payments.
- **Service Fee**: Additional charges for specialized services or expedited requests.
- **Late Fee**: Applied when invoices are not paid by the due date.

## **Setting Up Fees**

1. Navigate to **Pricing Settings** in Estizee.
2. Select **Fees & Convenience Charges**.
3. Define the fee type, amount, or percentage.
4. Specify conditions for application (e.g., payment method, service type).
5. Save settings to apply fees to future quotes and invoices.

## **Customer Communication**

- Clearly display all fees on quotes and invoices.
- Explain the purpose of each fee to maintain transparency.
- Include fees in total calculations automatically for accurate billing.

## **Best Practices**

- Keep fees reasonable and justifiable to avoid customer dissatisfaction.
- Review fees periodically to ensure compliance with regulations.
- Combine convenience charges with payment options for clarity.
- Ensure all staff understand when and how fees are applied.

Proper setup of fees and convenience charges in Estizee ensures transparent billing, fair compensation for services, and smooth payment processing.
