These settings help attract new customers, reward repeat business, and provide flexible pricing for special promotions.

All coupon configurations apply to **future quotes and jobs only**.

## **Types of Discounts**

Estizee supports several types of discounts:

- **Percentage-based**: Reduces the total by a specified percentage.
- **Flat-rate**: Subtracts a fixed amount from the total.
- **One-time or recurring**: Can be applied once or multiple times based on conditions.

## **Creating a Coupon**

When creating a coupon, you define:

- Coupon code or name
- Discount type and value
- Validity dates
- Usage limits (per customer or total)

## **Applying Coupons**

Coupons can be applied:

- Manually during quote creation
- Automatically based on predefined rules

Applied coupons adjust the final quote total and are reflected on invoices and customer communications.

## **Expiration & Restrictions**

Each coupon can have:

- Start and end dates
- Minimum order or service thresholds
- Restrictions for specific services or customer types

## **Best Practices**

- Keep codes simple and memorable
- Clearly define eligibility and expiration
- Monitor coupon usage to ensure profitability
- Combine with other promotional strategies thoughtfully

Proper configuration of Coupons & Discounts ensures consistent, fair, and strategic promotional pricing within Estizee.
