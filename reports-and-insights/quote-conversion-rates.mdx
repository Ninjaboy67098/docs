Monitoring conversion rates provides insights into sales performance and helps optimize pricing, marketing, and customer engagement strategies.

All features apply to **new quotes and jobs**.

## **Key Metrics**

- **Total Quotes Sent**: The number of quotes generated and shared with customers.
- **Approved Quotes**: The number of quotes that the customer has accepted.
- **Conversion Rate**: Calculated as `(Approved Quotes ÷ Total Quotes Sent) × 100%`.

## **Viewing Conversion Rates**

- Access conversion reports in the **Analytics** or **Reports** module.
- Filter by date range, service type, team member, or location.
- Identify trends in customer approval rates over time.

## **Best Practices**

- Track conversion rates regularly to evaluate sales effectiveness.
- Analyze reasons for declined quotes to improve pricing, communication, or service offerings.
- Segment conversion data by service type, customer type, or marketing channel for detailed insights.
- Use insights from conversion rates to refine quoting strategies and increase business performance.

Proper monitoring of quote conversion rates in Estizee helps identify opportunities for improvement, optimize pricing strategies, and maximize revenue.
