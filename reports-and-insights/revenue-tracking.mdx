---

Effective revenue tracking ensures accurate financial management, business insights, and performance monitoring.

All features apply to **completed jobs and invoices**.

## **Key Metrics**

- **Total Revenue**: Total income received from paid invoices.
- **Pending Revenue**: Income from approved quotes or invoices that have not yet been paid.
- **Revenue by Service**: Breakdown of income by service type (e.g., window cleaning, pressure washing).
- **Revenue by Team Member**: Tracks performance and contribution of individual employees.
- **Revenue by Customer**: Tracks income generated per customer.

## **Viewing Revenue**

- Access revenue reports in the **Analytics** or **Reports** module.
- Filter by date range, service type, team member, or location.
- Export reports for accounting or management purposes.

## **Best Practices**

- Regularly review revenue reports to track performance and identify trends.
- Compare actual revenue to goals and projections for insights into business growth.
- Investigate discrepancies promptly to maintain accurate financial records.
- Use revenue data to inform pricing, staffing, and marketing decisions.

Proper revenue tracking in Estizee ensures accurate financial management, identifies growth opportunities, and helps maintain a profitable business.
