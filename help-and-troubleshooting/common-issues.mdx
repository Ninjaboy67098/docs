---

Addressing these issues proactively ensures smooth operations and minimizes disruptions.

## **1. Quotes Not Sending**

- **Cause**: Incorrect customer email/phone, lack of SMS consent, or system connectivity issues.
- **Solution**: Verify customer contact info, confirm SMS/email consent, and check network connection.

## **2. Jobs Not Appearing in Calendar**

- **Cause**: Job status not set to Scheduled, or calendar sync issue.
- **Solution**: Confirm job status is Scheduled and verify Google/Apple Calendar sync settings.

## **3. Payment Failures**

- **Cause**: Incorrect payment details, expired card, or payment processor issues.
- **Solution**: Confirm payment information with customer and retry. Check payment processor dashboard for errors.

## **4. Notifications Not Sent**

- **Cause**: Notification settings not enabled, or customer opted out.
- **Solution**: Verify notification preferences and customer consent for SMS/email alerts.

## **5. Team Member Assignment Conflicts**

- **Cause**: Overlapping jobs or unavailable team member.
- **Solution**: Check team availability and adjust assignments to resolve conflicts.

## **6. Reports Not Updating**

- **Cause**: System delay or incorrect filters applied.
- **Solution**: Refresh reports, check filters, and ensure jobs/invoices are marked completed/paid.

## **Best Practices**

- Review common issues regularly to prevent recurring problems.
- Keep contact information, schedules, and payment methods up to date.
- Use system logs and support documentation to troubleshoot effectively.
- Train team members on proper use of Estizee to minimize errors.

Proactively managing common issues ensures a smooth experience for both administrators and team members, maintaining operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.
