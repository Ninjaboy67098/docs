## Common Causes

- **Incorrect Customer Contact Info**: Email address or phone number may be entered incorrectly.
- **Consent Not Granted**: Customer has not opted in to receive SMS or email notifications.
- **System or Network Issues**: Connectivity problems or server downtime.
- **Email Spam/Filters**: Emails may be blocked or routed to the recipient's spam folder.

## Resolution Steps

1. **Verify Customer Information**: Double-check the email and phone number provided.
2. **Confirm Consent**: Ensure the customer has opted in for the chosen communication method.
3. **Check System Status**: Ensure Estizee services are operational and your network connection is stable.
4. **Resend the Quote**: Attempt to resend via email or SMS.
5. **Alternative Delivery**: Send a PDF manually or provide the quote through another approved method.

## Best Practices

- Regularly validate customer contact information before sending quotes.
- Confirm communication consent during quote creation.
- Monitor system status and notifications to quickly identify issues.
- Document any failed quote attempts and resolutions to track recurring problems.

Ensuring quotes are successfully sent in Estizee maintains professional communication, avoids delays, and improves customer experience.
