---
title: "Incorrect Property Data"
---

We automatically look up your property's details — like square footage, number of floors, and property type — using public records instead of asking you to measure everything yourself. This makes getting a quote much faster.

However, public records aren't always up to date. If you've renovated, added an addition, or your home was recently built or divided into units, the records on file may not reflect your property's actual current size or layout.

**What this means for you:** Your estimate might be a bit higher or lower than expected if the on-file details don't match your home.

**What you can do:** If something looks off (like the square footage seems way too big or small), you can switch to entering your property details manually for a more accurate quote. Your final price will always be confirmed before any work begins.
