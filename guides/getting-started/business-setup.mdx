## 3. Residential Rate Settings

Configure how residential jobs are priced.

Common inputs include:

- Price per square foot
- Minimum service charge
- Story / height multipliers
- Add-ons (screens, tracks, deep cleaning, first-time service, etc.)

Each toggle or input in the estimator is directly tied to these values.

## 4. Commercial Rate Settings

Designed for storefronts, offices, buildings, and multi-unit properties.

You can configure:

- Per-window or per-pane pricing
- Square footage pricing
- Frequency discounts (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)

Commercial pricing can be more flexible and is often paired withÂ **Advanced Quotes**.

## 5. Pressure Washing Rates (coming soon)

If pressure washing is enabled, configure:

- Surface-based pricing (driveways, patios, sidewalks, walls)
- Square footage or flat-rate pricing
- Heavy soil / oil stain surcharges
- Minimum service thresholds

These rates integrate seamlessly with bundled quotes.

---

## 6. Notifications & Messaging

Control how and when customers are contacted.

Settings include:

- SMS notifications (quote sent, appointment confirmed, reminders)
- Email notifications (quotes, invoices, receipts)
- Opt-in consent handling for SMS (10DLC compliance)

> Customers will only receive messages they have explicitly consented to. 10DLC compliance usually takes a few business days to get accepted to start using SMS.

---

## 7. Team Management

Manage who can access your system and what they can do.

You can:

- Add team members
- Assign roles (Admin, Manager, Technician)

---

## 8. Calendar Settings

Customize how scheduling works.

Options include:

- Business hours
- Buffer time between jobs
- Available service days
- Default job duration estimates

These settings ensure accurate scheduling and prevent overbooking.

---

## 9. Coupons & Discounts

Create and manage promotional offers:

- Percentage discounts
- Flat-rate discounts
- One-time or recurring promotions
- Expiration dates

Coupons can be applied manually or automatically during quoting.
